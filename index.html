Eight great beaches in Skåne. For when the sun finally shines...

Skåne is the southernmost county of Sweden. Its climate is more milder than the raw one with which we usually associate Scandinavia. Thanks to this, in summertime the local beaches are willingly visited by plenty of locals and visitors. One of Skåne’s most popular attractions is of course its beaches, of which there are luckily plenty. A car is a perfect way to get around, but if you like the idea of whizzing past wheat fields with the wind in your hair, you could rent a bike to explore Skåne. There is also a great public transport system, that takes you wherever you want. Not a local? Visit Hotels.com to find a place to stay!

No matter your taste in beach, Skåne obliges. From the wild, wind-lashed shores and snuggled-away coves of Kullaberg to exotic Knäbäckshusen, we bring you the cream of the Skåne coast with these top 8 beaches.
